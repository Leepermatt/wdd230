Visual Hierarchy

Coca Cola

coca-cola.com

This uses type face along with color contrast to display a visual hierarchy. It grabs the eye and quickly communicates the desired information. It also uses proximity to display Coke next to food to make you think the drink goes with a meal

Fitt's Law

Nike

Nike.com

Nike.com uses Fitt's law very well. When the website loads your cursor is located right on a link to purchase the items that displayed. You don't even have to move the mouse to be able to click the link to taek you to their products. Fitt's law states that "that the time to move to a target depends on how big it is and on how far away it is".
